"Read Euler, read Euler, he is the master of us all." - Pierre-Simon Laplace

Our Motivation

Euler's Challenges was born from a deep passion for mathematics and a desire to honor one of history's greatest mathematicians, Leonhard Euler. Our competition aims to inspire the next generation of mathematical minds by presenting challenging problems that require creativity, logical thinking, and mathematical elegance.

We believe that mathematics is not just about calculations, but about discovering patterns, solving puzzles, and finding beauty in abstract concepts. Through this competition, we hope to foster a love for mathematics and encourage students to push the boundaries of their mathematical understanding.

Competition Rules & Procedures

"Nothing takes place in the world whose meaning is not that of some maximum or minimum." - Leonhard Euler

General Rules

  • Open to students aged 14-22
  • Individual competition only
  • No calculators or electronic devices allowed
  • All solutions must be written in English or French
  • Partial credit awarded for incomplete solutions

Competition Format

  • Duration: 4 hours
  • 6 problems of increasing difficulty
  • Each problem worth 7 points (42 total)
  • Problems cover various mathematical areas
  • Emphasis on proof and reasoning

Scoring & Awards

  • Gold Medal: Top 5% of participants
  • Silver Medal: Next 10% of participants
  • Bronze Medal: Next 15% of participants
  • Honorable Mention: Next 20% of participants
  • Special prizes for elegant solutions

Registration Process

  • Online registration required
  • Registration fee: €25
  • Deadline: 4 weeks before competition
  • Confirmation email with venue details
  • Valid ID required on competition day
